## 📖 Business Case
**Atlas Office Supplies & Furniture** was founded with a vision to provide modern, reliable, and affordable workspace solutions.Our mission is to empower businesses and individuals with high-quality office products that enhance productivity, comfort, and creativity.

This project transforms raw operational data (9,994 records) into an interactive strategic tool to empower decision-making. The dashboard is designed according to the Atlas brand identity, utilizing the official color palette:
**Primary Blue (#1E3A8A)**: Trust and Professionalism.
  **Accent Orange (#F97316)**: Innovation and Energy.
  **Neutral Gray (#6B7280)**: Balance and Practicality.

---

## 🎯 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks four primary metrics to monitor the "pulse" of the business:
1. **Total Sales**: Gross revenue generated across the entire dataset.
2.**Total Profit**: The bottom-line earnings of the company.
3.**Average Order Value (AOV)**: The average revenue generated per order.
4. **Customer Count**: Total unique customers served.

---

## 🔍 Strategic Insights
The dashboard answers critical business questions to guide next-quarter strategy:
***Seasonality**: A Year-over-Year (YoY) line chart validates seasonal trends, identifying peaks in Q3 and Q4 to manage inventory
* **Regional Health**: Identifies regions with the highest sales records and states that require strategic investment to boost margins
* **Segment Strategy**: Analyzes customer segments that appear less profitable despite high sales volume, implying potential issues with discounting or operational costs
* **Logistics Impact**: Visualizes how shipping costs and delivery methods impact customer satisfaction and overall profitability
