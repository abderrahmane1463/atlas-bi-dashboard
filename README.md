## 🎯 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
[cite_start]The dashboard tracks four primary metrics to monitor the "pulse" of the business[cite: 32]:
1. **Total Sales**: Gross revenue generated across the entire dataset.
2. [cite_start]**Total Profit**: The bottom-line earnings of the company[cite: 21].
3. [cite_start]**Average Order Value (AOV)**: The average revenue generated per order[cite: 24].
4. [cite_start]**Customer Count**: Total unique customers served[cite: 22].

---

## 🔍 Strategic Insights
[cite_start]The dashboard answers critical business questions to guide next-quarter strategy[cite: 16]:
* [cite_start]**Seasonality**: A Year-over-Year (YoY) line chart validates seasonal trends, identifying peaks in Q3 and Q4 to manage inventory[cite: 27].
* [cite_start]**Regional Health**: Identifies regions with the highest sales records and states that require strategic investment to boost margins[cite: 20, 28].
* [cite_start]**Segment Strategy**: Analyzes customer segments that appear less profitable despite high sales volume, implying potential issues with discounting or operational costs[cite: 26].
* [cite_start]**Logistics Impact**: Visualizes how shipping costs and delivery methods impact customer satisfaction and overall profitability[cite: 29].
